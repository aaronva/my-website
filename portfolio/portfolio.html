Marvel Avenger's Commander Deck

This is a Commander Deck I am building drawing inspiration from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. I've played the deck a couple times with my friends, and I discovered that the deck still needs some cohesion. There are definitely a couple cool cards that should inspire their own decks, but I really want this deck to be viable on it's own. Fortunately there are a couple empty slots in the deck that can serve make the deck function as a whole, and the commander himself can get a tweak to push that direction.
